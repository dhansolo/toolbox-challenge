Memory Game

Created By David Han

How To Play

Click on a square panel to start the game. The timer will start once you click on one panel.

Once you click on two panels, if they match, the pictures will stay flipped.

If they do not match, both panels flip back after one second.

There are counters at the top that keeps track of how many pairs
you have found, how many pairs you have guessed wrong, and how many pairs are left.

Once you found all of the pairs, you will be rewarded for your success
